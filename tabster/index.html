FEATURES

Tabster is a chrome extension that helps you multitask effortlessly in your browser.

  • Open multiple website
    You can open multiple websites in same tab.
  • Flexible Plugins
    Video player, Notepad etc. You can even add your own plugins.
  • Customizable Layouts
    Choose layout, customize according to your need.
  • Open Source
    You can see the code, modify according to your needs.
